About Us

Pioneering the Future of Technology

AutoVIT is a cutting-edge technical club at VIT that brings together passionate minds in robotics, automation, automobile engineering, and electronics. We are a community of innovators, creators, and problem-solvers dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology.

Through hands-on projects, workshops, and participation in national-level competitions, we provide our members with the practical experience and knowledge needed to excel in the rapidly evolving world of technology.

Our Domains

Robotics

Design and build autonomous robots, participate in competitions, and explore AI-driven robotics solutions.

  • Autonomous Navigation
  • Robot Operating System (ROS)
  • Computer Vision
  • Machine Learning Integration

Automation

Develop industrial automation solutions, IoT systems, and smart manufacturing processes.

  • PLC Programming
  • Industrial IoT
  • Process Control
  • Smart Manufacturing

Automobile

Work on electric vehicles, autonomous driving systems, and automotive electronics.

  • Electric Vehicle Design
  • Autonomous Systems
  • Engine Management
  • Vehicle Dynamics

Electronics

Create embedded systems, develop PCBs, and work with cutting-edge electronic components.

  • Embedded Systems
  • PCB Design
  • Microcontrollers
  • Signal Processing
